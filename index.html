About Thulium

Rare by nature.
Scarce by design.

Thulium is one of the rarest lanthanide elements on Earth. THULIUM the token mirrors that scarcity — a fixed supply of 1,000,000 tokens, no minting, no inflation, launched fairly on the Base network.
